Dungeons & Dragons

Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) is a legendary fantasy tabletop role-playing game where players create unique characters and embark on epic adventures guided by a storyteller called the Dungeon Master. Set in magical worlds filled with dragons, dungeons, and mythical creatures, players use imagination, strategy, and dice rolls to overcome challenges and shape their own heroic stories.
